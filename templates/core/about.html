Our Story

DaFlavors was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to connect people in Roxas City with the perfect venues and catering services for their important events. We recognized that planning events can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to finding the right venue and menu that fits your vision and budget.

Our platform brings together the finest private dining spaces and professional caterers in the region, making it easy to discover, compare, and book the perfect combination for any occasion. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate function, milestone birthday, or any special celebration, DaFlavors is your trusted partner.

Our Mission

At DaFlavors, we strive to be the premier online marketplace for discovering, booking, and planning private dining events and catered functions in Roxas City and eventually Western Visayas. We aim to empower both clients and service providers with seamless tools for customization and efficient management.

We believe that every event should be memorable, and that starts with the perfect venue and delicious food. Our platform makes the planning process transparent, reliable, and stress-free.
